The Queen Of Sheba Restaurant

  • Approximate price pp: 25 €
  • Environment: Ideal for a family lunch or dinner.
  • Google reviews: 4.8 stars
  • Wheelchair friendly
  • Phone: 0611841997
  • Find in Maps

Description

This Ethiopian food restaurant is in a discreet location inside a community center, offering mostly vegetarian food, but it can also be vegan. The decoration is really beautiful, and it's mostly operated by one person, making you feel welcome. The food is delicious, and they can serve up to four portions in one plate, which is very interesting.

Vegan Bakery / Zaandijk

  • Approximate price pp: 12 €
  • Environment: Ideal for everyone, perfect for morning coffee and to get home bread.
  • Google reviews: 4.7 stars
  • Wheelchair friendly
  • Phone: 0756281719
  • Find in Maps

Description

This bakery is where the locals from the village go on a Sunday morning to pick up the bread for a nice breakfast. Everybody is so friendly here! The bread they sell is almost art. It's mainly vegetarian but they have a few vegan options as well. Pastries and cakes are all over the shop. The coffee they prepare is delicious, maybe one of the best I have tried. Sad news, it is closed on Sundays.
