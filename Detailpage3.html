“Are we there yet?” If you are traveling with kids or if you simply enjoy interactive places with your family, you are in the right path. Enjoy a new and educative experience that everyone will love.

Sea Life Scheveningen / Den Haag

  • Approximate price pp: 20 €
  • Environment: Suitable for all groups and age.
  • Google reviews: 4.0 stars
  • Wheelchair friendly
  • Phone: 0703542100
  • Find in Maps

Description

This is a big aquarium chain present in different countries, but this is the only one in The Netherlands, and it's ideal to spend time with kids or a large group of people, not suggested for vegans though. They have around nine sections of animals in captivity and it is possible to interact with some of them.

Zoo Animal Farm / Beverwijk

  • Approximate price pp: 00.00 € (donations are welcome)
  • Environment: Suitable for all groups and age, perfect for kids.
  • Google reviews: 4.3 stars
  • Wheelchair friendly
  • Phone: 0614997387
  • Find in Maps

Description

This small-size zoo is an amazing place to spend some educational time with the kids! It has a lot of activities and animals that the small ones will enjoy, and it is possible to interact with some of the beautiful species. This is a non-profit establishment whose main purpose is to support the balance between animals and humans in the new fast-growing world. Lot to learn here!
