”Lekker”, a Dutch adjective for delicious, highly pleasing to the taste. Pleasant, enjoyable. Here you will read about some of the best vegan places we have found so far.

Mica Cafe Bar / Haarlem

  • Approximate price pp: 11 €
  • Environment: all ages, great for a date or a morning cafe.
  • Happy Cow: 4.5 stars
  • Wheelchair friendly
  • Phone: Not available
  • Find in Maps

Description

At Mica, you will find the most tasty croissants I have ever tried so far: a normal croissant with a matcha layer on top and an almond butter filling. They also have delicious cookies, brownies, and banana and carrot bread.

The drinks selection is also amazing. They have matcha latte, cold brew, and oat milk is always available. This cafe is pet-friendly and attracts all kinds of people, from solo readers to families.

Vegan Sushi Bar

  • Approximate price pp: 21 €
  • Environment: young adults.
  • Happy Cow: 5.0 stars
  • Wheelchair friendly
  • Phone: Not available
  • Find in Maps

Description

This is a small chain, with two locations in Amsterdam and one in Arnhem. They serve delicious sushi, soups, salads, and some amazing desserts.

The “Boxes” they can put together are the best; they contain variety and a good amount of rolls. The tea they serve is a must when coming to this restaurant. The vibe is perfect for a date or a meal with friends.
