Zaandam

  • Environment: Ideal for everyone and anyone.
  • Find in Maps

Description

With around 76,804 residents nowadays, the history of Zaandam and the surrounding Zaan River region (the Zaanstreek) is intimately tied to industry. In the Dutch Golden Age, Zaandam served as a large milling centre. Thousands of windmills powered saws that processed Scandinavian wood for the shipbuilding and paper industries. A statue that commemorates this industry was commissioned from sculptor Slavomir Miletić, ”De houtwerker” ("The Woodworker"), was installed on 20 June 2004. This is an amazing village, and the very peculiar and famous hotel is here, the “Inntel Hotels Zaandam”, with its creative design overlapping green wooden facades typical of traditional houses in the region. Designed by Molenaar & Van Winden architecten, possibly has more visitors due to the façade than to the morning breakfast.

Texel

  • Environment: Ideal for everyone and anyone.
  • Find in Maps

Description

Texel is an island located in the northwest of the country, around 13,656 people is registered as living there. This beautiful off-the-coast place is one of the most beautiful places to visit, is well known for its variety of bird habitats: the North Sea, the tidal Wadden Sea, sand dunes, lagoons, bays, woods, wet and dry polderlands and meadow lands. Hundreds of thousands of birds gather on the Wadden Sea during bird migration. The island is easy to explore with a (rental) bicycle and interesting birds can be seen every season. It stretches 20 km in length and averages 8 km in width, so it is easy to explore by yourself.
